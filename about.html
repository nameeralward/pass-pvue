About Us

Our journey began in 2024 when a group of passionate pharmacists and tech enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to revolutionize how pharmacists prepare for the OMSB Pearson Vue Exam. Having experienced the challenges of exam preparation firsthand, we understood the need for a more intelligent, adaptive, and comprehensive study solution.

What started as a simple question bank has evolved into a sophisticated learning platform, incorporating cutting-edge AI technology and detailed analytics. We've continuously refined our content and features based on user feedback and the latest developments in clinical therapeutics and Omani practice guidelines.

Today, passPVUE represents the culmination of countless hours of development, testing, and improvement. We're proud to help pharmacy professionals achieve success on the OMSB Pearson Vue Exam, and we remain committed to our mission of making high-quality exam preparation accessible to all.
