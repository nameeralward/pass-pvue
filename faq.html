Frequently Asked Questions

What is passPVUE?

passPVUE is a comprehensive study companion app designed specifically for pharmacy professionals preparing for the OMSB Pearson Vue Exam. It offers over 2,000 practice questions, AI-powered assistance, and various study modes to optimize your exam preparation.

How do I purchase a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Simply download the app and select your preferred access plan (3 months for $69 or 6 months for $99) within the app. All payments are processed securely through Apple's payment system.

Can I use passPVUE on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use passPVUE on multiple Apple devices with the same Apple ID. However, to prevent account sharing, we implement a device switching cooldown system. Please refer to our Device Switching Policy for details.

Is my progress saved across devices?

Yes, all your progress, bookmarks, and study data are synchronized across your devices when you're signed in with the same account.

How often is the content updated?

We regularly update our question bank and content to ensure accuracy and relevance. Updates are automatically delivered through the App Store.

What happens when my subscription expires?

When your subscription expires, you'll maintain access to your progress data but won't be able to access practice questions or study materials until you renew your subscription.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How does the AI assistance work?

Our AI system analyzes your performance and study patterns to provide personalized explanations, identify knowledge gaps, and suggest focused study areas. It also helps generate detailed answer rationales and summaries for therapeutic topics.

Is passPVUE available on Android devices?

Currently, passPVUE is exclusively available for Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, and Mac). While we don't have an Android version at the moment, we are exploring the possibility of developing one in the future to make our platform more accessible.

For now, you can use passPVUE on:

  • iPhone
  • iPad
  • Mac

What topics are covered in the OMSB Pearson Vue Exam?

The OMSB Pearson Vue Exam covers a wide range of therapeutic topics including:

  • Clinical Pharmacy Practice
  • Pharmaceutical Care
  • Medication Therapy Management
  • Pharmacy Law and Ethics in Oman
  • Patient Safety and Quality
  • Evidence-Based Medicine
  • Public Health and Health Promotion
  • Healthcare Systems in Oman
  • Research Methods and Statistics
  • Professional Development
